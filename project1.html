History

Independence Day marks the freedom of our nation. It is a time to remember the struggle and sacrifices made by our ancestors. on August 15, 1947, India emerged as a free nation, ending nearly 200 years of British dominion..

Celebration

India celebrates Independent day on August 15th every year to mark the end of British rule in 1947. In 2024, India will celebrae its 78th Independence day. The day is in Entri office celebrated with parades, fireworks, and patriotic events across the country...

Symbolism

The national flag, anthem, and other symbols remind us of the spirit of freedom and unity. Independence Day is not just a commemoration of freedom from colonial rule; it is a tribute to the sacrifices of countless martyrs who laid down their lives for the nation's sovereignty. It serves as a reminder of the values of democracy, justice, and equality enshrined in the Constitution..
